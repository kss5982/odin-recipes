Soldier's Kisses

Description:

These cookies are so good and so easy to make! The recipe came from the WWII era.

Ingredients:

  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cups finely chopped walnuts

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Beat the egg whites until light and frothy (~3-4 minutes). Beat in sugar about 1 tablespoon at a time until mixed.
  3. Continue mixing until mixture is thick and shiny. ~2 more minutes.
  4. Fold in walnuts into the mixutre. Drop heaping teaspoonful onto the baking sheet.
  5. Bake until dry to the touch. Approximately 40 minutes.
