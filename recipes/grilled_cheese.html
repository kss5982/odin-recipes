Spicy Grilled Cheese Recipe:

Description:

Grilled cheese sandwiches are cheap, delicious, and easy to make. However, they tend to be bland. With this recipe, you can add some kick to this popular dish and be pleasntly surprised by the outcome!

We'll first list the ingredient on how to make this wonderful dish. Then, we'll tell you the exact steps required to produce it.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 4 slices white bread
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 1 roma (plum) tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 small onion, chopped
  • 1 jalanapeno pepper, chopped

Steps:

  1. Heat a large skillet over low heat.
  2. Spread butter/margarine onto one side of 2 slices of bread. Place both pieces buttered side down in the skillet.
  3. Add a slice of cheese on each slice. Top with tomato, onion, and jalanapeno slices.
  4. Butter 1 side of the the remaining slices and place on top buttered side up.
  5. When the bottom of the sandwiches are toasted, flip and fry until brown on the other side.
