Cereal Cookies!

Description:

Chewy meringue-style cookies with crispy rice cereal and coconut folded in.

Ingredients:

  • 2 egg whites:
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 cups high-protein crisp rice and wheat cereal
  • 1 cup flaked coconut
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease cookie sheets or line with parchment paper.
  2. Beat egg whites until soft peaks form in a clean and dry glass/metal bowl.
  3. Fold in cereal, coconut, and vanilla. Drop heaping spoonfuls onto the cookie sheets.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes in the preheated oven until firm. Cool before removing from cookie sheets.
